Welcome to Laguna's Best!


Laguna's Best offers delicacies from the province of Laguna. We show the most famous dessert and snack recipes.



Recipe Categories

Buko Pie

Buko pie is a Filipino-style coconut pie made of fresh coconut meat combined with a creamy filling and in a flaky pie crust.

Bibingka de Macapuno

Bibingka is a 'kakanin' or a rice cake that's primarily made with rice flour

Espasol

Espasol soft and chewy. Made of toasted rice flour and sweetened coconut milk.

Kesong Puti

Kesong puti is a Filipino soft, unaged, white cheese made from unskimmed carabao milk

Monay

Monay, also known as pan de monja, is a dense bread roll made with all-purpose flour, milk, and salt.

Ube Halaya

Ube halaya or halayang ube is made from boiled and mashed purple yam
